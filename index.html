Jenny Nelson

is a writer, artist, and community gardener living in Brooklyn, NY.

View her resume here.

Jenny has over ten years of professional experience writing for a global online audience, from humor pieces and interviews to UX copy.

Her most concise writing includes marketing copy for the online learning platform Codecademy and creative content for the mobile app Google Primer and weather chatbot Poncho.

And here are some examples of slightly longer work (more available on request):
Catherine Cohen profile for FADER / Birthday Boys interview for Videogum / 4 steps to more inclusive, precise language for Think with Google


For personal creative fulfillment Jenny makes music with the projects Concourse, Grassbrothers, and LLB, among others.

She also has collaborated on multimedia performances and installations including Jenny, World Group, and the online meditation WhatsHappening.Click with Luiza Dale; a suite of dating app features with Patrick Hosmer; and an interactive cell phone-charging station with K. Kovacs.
